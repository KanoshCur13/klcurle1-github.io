How the player grows and improves their skills over time.

What is Progression?

Progression in games refers to the process by which players advance through levels, gain experience, and unlock new abilities or content. Allowing players to grow stronger over time through experience points is a fundamental aspect of game design that keeps players engaged and motivated to continue playing.

Common Systems

These include leveling systems, skills trees, and achievement systems. Or even equipment upgrades.

Benefits of Progression

Progression provides a sense of accomplishment and keeps players engaged with the game. It encourages replayability and allows players to see their improvement over time.

Quick Facts

  • Progression equals player growth and advancement
  • Common systems: leveling, skill trees, achievements
  • Benefits: sense of accomplishment, engagement, replayability
