Comparison of Game Mechanics

How combat, progression, and randomness interact in games.

Mechanic Purpose Impact
Combat Engage players in conflict and challenge their skills. Provides excitement and tests player abilities.
Progression Allow players to grow and improve over time. Encourages player engagement and motivation.
Randomness Add unpredictability and variety to gameplay. Enhances replayability and keeps players engaged.
